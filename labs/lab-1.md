## Materials

For this lab, you will need:

- 1 Arduino Nano
- 1 Breadboard
- 9 LEDs

- 3 7.5k$$\Omega$$ resistors
- 3 10k$$\Omega$$ resistors

As well a computer with the Arduino IDE [installed](/tutorials/#arduino-ide-install) and [setup](/tutorials/#arduino-library).
